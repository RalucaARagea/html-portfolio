The Best Movies according to Raluca

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Emperor's new groove

This is my absolute favourite movie. It has everything I need to cheer up on a blue day. It never fails to make me laugh even though I saw it like a million times already.

Everything everywhere all at once

I cannot describe this movie. You just have to see it.

In time

Looking back at this movie makes me realize that it could've been an episode of Black Mirror. The mechanics of how time works here just make sense to me in an existential kind of way. It puts in perpective that old saying: "Time is money".
